Get Started

Your practical checklist for hitting the courts.

Essential Gear

The best part about starting is how little you need. You don't need fancy outfits or expensive gear. All you truly need is a paddle and a ball.

For shoes, don't buy anything special to start. Any athletic shoes or tennis-like shoes with good side-to-side support will work perfectly.
